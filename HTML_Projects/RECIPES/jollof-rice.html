JOLLOF RICE

Jollof (/dʒəˈlɒf/), or jollof rice, is a rice dish from Senegal, West Africa.[3] It is typically made with long-grain rice, tomatoes, chilis, onions, spices, and sometimes other vegetables and/or meat in a single pot, although its ingredients and preparation methods vary across different regions.
